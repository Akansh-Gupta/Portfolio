PROJECT 03 : CheckItOff
    Keep on top of everything with CheckItOff, a comprehensive task management application integrating to-do lists, notes, and reminders. Data is stored locally within your browser for seamless data retention across sessions. Manage your to-dos by marking tasks as complete, undoing completions, or deleting them. Organize your notes with descriptive titles for easy searching and retrieval. Set timely reminders for upcoming events and receive notifications so you never miss a deadline. CheckItOff empowers you to stay organized and boost your productivity with its intuitive and streamlined interface.
