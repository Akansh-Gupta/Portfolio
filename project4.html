PROJECT 04 : Digi Diary
    Digi-Diary is a user-friendly blogging platform designed for content creation, sharing, and community interaction. Users can easily publish and manage personal blogs on diverse topics. Readers can engage with content through comments and discussions, fostering a vibrant online community of writers and readers. Powered by EJS for server-side rendering, Digi-Diary ensures efficient page delivery and a smooth user experience. It provides a space for individuals to share their thoughts, connect with others, and explore a variety of content in a dynamic online environment.
