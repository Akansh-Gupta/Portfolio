PROJECT 07 : 7 Wonders of the World
    Explore the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World through this interactive project. Delve into their rich history, stunning architecture, and cultural significance with engaging text and visuals. This project offers unique features, including integrated Google Maps to pinpoint each wonder's location and Street View imagery for immersive virtual tours. Uncover the ingenuity of past civilizations and experience these remarkable structures firsthand. From the Great Pyramid to the Lighthouse of Alexandria, embark on a journey through time and discover the enduring legacy of these ancient marvels.
