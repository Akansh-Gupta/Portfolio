PROJECT 02 : Eat Street
    Eat Street simplifies online food ordering with a user-friendly platform. Explore a diverse, categorized menu and use the search function to quickly find your desired dishes. Easily customize your order, adjusting quantities and adding special instructions directly in the cart. Eat Street maintains a comprehensive order history for each user, allowing for convenient reordering of past favorites. Enjoy a seamless and efficient experience, bringing a world of culinary options to your fingertips. With intuitive navigation and a focus on convenience, Eat Street makes satisfying your cravings easier than ever.
