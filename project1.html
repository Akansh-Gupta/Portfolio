PROJECT 01 : Couch Critic
    Couch Critic is a dynamic web platform built with React.js for cinema enthusiasts to connect and explore the world of film. Users can post reviews, rate movies and performances, and engage in community discussions, all powered by MongoDB. Leveraging the IMDb API, Couch Critic provides comprehensive movie information, including plot summaries, cast details, ratings, and financial data. Its user-friendly interface and intuitive navigation create a vibrant online community for sharing opinions and delving deep into cinematic artistry.
