PROJECT 05 : The Buzz
    The Buzz is a comprehensive news website designed to provide global coverage across diverse domains, including business, sports, science, technology, and general news. Utilizing a robust news API, the platform aggregates current events from around the world, ensuring access to up-to-date information. Key features of The Buzz include intuitive news categorization, enabling users to efficiently navigate and locate relevant content based on their interests. This categorization system enhances the user experience by streamlining information discovery. The website's design prioritizes accessibility and readability, offering a user-friendly interface for seamless browsing. The Buzz successfully creates a valuable resource for staying informed on global events.
